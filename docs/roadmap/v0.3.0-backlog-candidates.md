# v0.3.0 Backlog Candidates

## Must Have

- External email provider integration with safe secret configuration.
- Notification delivery retry and failure tracking.
- Certificate/completion evidence requirements and domain model design.
- Analytics event taxonomy for learner activity and content operations.
- Production readiness improvements from v0.2.0 lessons learned.

## Should Have

- Certificate issuing workflow for completed courses or learning milestones.
- Admin certificate verification and learner certificate view.
- Delivery monitoring for email and notifications.
- Content authoring quality-of-life improvements for lessons, quizzes, and publishing.
- Staging/production smoke validation scripts.

## Could Have

- AI Tutor preparation spike focused on safety, grounding, costs, and evaluation.
- External search provider spike and indexing design.
- PWA/mobile readiness review.
- Teacher/admin collaboration concepts such as review assignments and comments.
- Enhanced learning analytics dashboards.

## Won't Have For v0.3.0

- Payment/subscription implementation.
- Marketplace implementation.
- Full AI tutor production rollout.
- Native mobile app.
- Leaderboard or social features.
- Advanced warehouse-scale analytics platform.
