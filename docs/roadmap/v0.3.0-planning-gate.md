# v0.3.0 Planning Gate

## Purpose

Define candidate themes for v0.3.0 planning. This document does not authorize implementation.

## Candidate Themes

| Theme | Business Value | User Value | Risk | Complexity | Dependency | Recommended Priority | Why Now / Why Later |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| AI Tutor | Differentiates product and increases engagement | Personalized help and feedback | High model, safety, cost, and UX risk | High | Prompting, content grounding, moderation, usage controls | Medium | Prepare foundations now; build later after analytics and content quality mature. |
| External Email Provider | Enables reliable notification delivery and account workflows | Users receive timely messages | Medium deliverability and secret-management risk | Medium | Provider selection, secrets, retry, bounce handling | High | Strong operational value and unlocks future account flows. |
| External Search Engine | Improves discovery at scale | Faster and richer content search | Medium indexing and consistency risk | Medium | Search provider, indexing pipeline, monitoring | Medium | Useful after content volume grows; local search may suffice short term. |
| Advanced Analytics | Supports product decisions and learning insights | Better recommendations and progress visibility | Medium privacy and reporting risk | Medium | Event quality, metrics definitions, storage | High | Helps prioritize future product work and validate learner outcomes. |
| Mobile/PWA | Expands access and retention | Better mobile learning experience | Medium browser/device compatibility risk | Medium | Responsive UX, offline strategy, installability | Medium | Valuable, but core workflows and release operations should stabilize first. |
| Payment | Enables monetization | Unlocks paid plans later | High compliance and support risk | High | Pricing, billing provider, tax/support policy | Low | Later; product-market and learning value should be stronger first. |
| Marketplace | Expands content ecosystem | More content options | High moderation and trust risk | High | Creator roles, payments, review workflow | Low | Later; requires mature content operations and trust model. |
| Certificate System | Creates completion evidence | Learners can prove progress | Medium integrity and anti-abuse risk | Medium | Course completion rules, identity, audit trail | High | Good v0.3.0 candidate because it builds on courses, quizzes, and reports. |
| Teacher/Admin Collaboration | Improves team workflows | Faster review and coordination | Medium workflow and permissions risk | Medium | Notifications, roles, comments, assignments | Medium | Useful after notification delivery is reliable. |
| Content Authoring Improvement | Speeds content creation and quality | Better lessons, quizzes, and study content | Medium scope creep risk | Medium | Editor UX, validation, quality checks | High | Strong immediate value and improves existing v0.2.0 modules. |

## Top 3 Recommended Directions

1. External Email Provider + Notification Delivery.
2. Certificate System / Learning Completion Evidence.
3. Advanced Analytics or AI Tutor Preparation.

## Gate Decision

Proceed to prioritization and scope slicing only. Do not implement v0.3.0 features until the release owner approves the selected scope.
