# v0.3.0 Decision Matrix

## Scoring

Scores use 1-5. Higher user/business impact is better. Higher risk/effort scores mean more cost or uncertainty. Total priority score is:

`User impact + Business impact - Technical risk - Development effort - Operational risk - Dependency risk`

## Matrix

| Theme | User Impact | Business Impact | Technical Risk | Development Effort | Operational Risk | Dependency Risk | Total Priority Score | Notes |
| --- | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: | --- |
| External Email Provider + Notification Delivery | 4 | 5 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 3 | -3 | Strong unlock for account and operational workflows. |
| Certificate System / Learning Completion Evidence | 4 | 4 | 3 | 3 | 2 | 3 | -3 | Clear learner value and builds on existing course/quiz/report modules. |
| Advanced Analytics Foundation | 4 | 4 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 3 | -4 | Supports roadmap decisions and future personalization. |
| Content Authoring Improvement | 3 | 4 | 2 | 3 | 2 | 2 | -2 | Good near-term productivity with lower operational risk. |
| AI Tutor Preparation | 5 | 5 | 5 | 5 | 5 | 4 | -9 | High upside, but should start as preparation/spike, not broad rollout. |
| External Search Engine | 4 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 3 | -5 | Useful when content grows; not the first bottleneck. |
| Mobile/PWA | 4 | 3 | 3 | 4 | 3 | 3 | -6 | Valuable but broad; consider after core flows and operations mature. |
| Teacher/Admin Collaboration | 3 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 2 | 3 | -5 | Better after notifications are reliable. |
| Payment | 3 | 5 | 5 | 5 | 5 | 5 | -12 | Monetization value, but high compliance/support burden. |
| Marketplace | 3 | 4 | 5 | 5 | 5 | 5 | -13 | Needs mature trust, moderation, creator, and payment systems. |

## Recommendation

Prioritize a balanced v0.3.0 slice:

1. External Email Provider + Notification Delivery.
2. Certificate System / Learning Completion Evidence.
3. Advanced Analytics Foundation, with AI Tutor preparation limited to discovery and risk reduction.
