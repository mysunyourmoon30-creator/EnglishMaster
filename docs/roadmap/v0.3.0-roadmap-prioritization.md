# v0.3.0 Roadmap Prioritization

## Status

`Planning only`

No v0.3.0 feature implementation is authorized by this document.

## Top 3 Recommended Themes

1. Certificate System / Learning Completion Evidence.
2. External Email Provider + Notification Delivery.
3. Content Authoring Improvement.

## Recommended v0.3.0 Scope

Recommended MVP slice:

- Add completion evidence and certificate foundations for course/lesson/quiz progress.
- Integrate an external email provider behind the existing email abstraction with safe secret handling, retry/failure tracking, and delivery monitoring.
- Improve content authoring workflows where they directly support certificate-ready courses and release-quality content.

Recommended planning spike:

- Advanced Analytics Foundation for learner and content operations event taxonomy.
- AI Tutor Preparation limited to safety, grounding, cost, and evaluation research, without production tutor rollout.

## Themes To Defer

- Payment: defer until pricing, support, compliance, and value proposition are ready.
- Marketplace: defer until trust, moderation, creator workflow, and payment systems are mature.
- Full AI Tutor rollout: defer until analytics, safety, grounding, moderation, and cost controls are ready.
- Mobile/PWA: defer broad work, but allow responsive fixes only if needed for selected v0.3.0 flows.
- External Search Engine: defer unless content volume or search quality becomes a measurable blocker.
- Teacher/Admin Collaboration: defer broad collaboration until notifications are reliable.

## Risks

- External email introduces deliverability, secrets, retry, and monitoring responsibilities.
- Certificates need clear completion rules, auditability, and anti-abuse thinking.
- Content authoring work can grow broad unless tied to v0.3.0 learning/completion goals.
- Analytics must avoid collecting sensitive auth data or private learner information without clear purpose.
- Starting AI Tutor too early could create safety, cost, privacy, and quality risks.

## Final Recommendation

Choose a narrow v0.3.0 slice that improves learner trust and operational maturity:

1. Certificate System / Learning Completion Evidence.
2. External Email Provider + Notification Delivery.
3. Content Authoring Improvement.

Keep Advanced Analytics and AI Tutor as preparation tracks, not full feature commitments.
