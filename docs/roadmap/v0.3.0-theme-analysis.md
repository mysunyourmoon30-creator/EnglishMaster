# v0.3.0 Theme Analysis

## Purpose

Compare candidate v0.3.0 themes before implementation. This is planning only.

## Analysis Table

| Theme | User Value | Business Value | Technical Complexity | Security Risk | Operational Risk | Development Effort | Dependency Risk | Maintenance Cost | Release Risk | Fit With v0.2.0 System | Analysis |
| --- | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: | --- |
| External Email Provider + Notification Delivery | 4 | 5 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 5 | Unlocks real delivery for existing notification/email foundation and future account workflows. |
| Certificate System / Learning Completion Evidence | 5 | 4 | 3 | 3 | 2 | 3 | 2 | 2 | 3 | 5 | Builds naturally on courses, lessons, quizzes, achievements, and weekly reports. |
| Advanced Analytics Foundation | 4 | 4 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 4 | Helps product decisions and future personalization, but needs careful privacy boundaries. |
| AI Tutor Preparation | 5 | 5 | 5 | 5 | 5 | 5 | 4 | 4 | 5 | 3 | High upside, but too risky for broad rollout without analytics, safety, grounding, and cost controls. |
| Mobile / PWA | 4 | 3 | 3 | 2 | 3 | 4 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 3 | Useful for learner access, but broad UX and device work could distract from operational gaps. |
| Payment | 3 | 5 | 5 | 5 | 5 | 5 | 5 | 5 | 5 | 2 | Monetization is valuable, but support, compliance, refunds, and trust are premature. |
| Marketplace | 3 | 4 | 5 | 5 | 5 | 5 | 5 | 5 | 5 | 2 | Requires moderation, creator trust, payments, policies, and mature content operations. |
| Teacher / Admin Collaboration | 3 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 4 | Valuable after reliable notifications and review workflows are in place. |
| Content Authoring Improvement | 4 | 4 | 2 | 2 | 2 | 3 | 2 | 3 | 2 | 5 | Strong fit with current admin/content modules and lowers content operations friction. |
| External Search Engine | 4 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 4 | 3 | 3 | 3 | Useful when content volume grows, but current public search can carry near-term needs. |

## Notes

- Security and operational risks are intentionally weighted heavily because v0.2.0 production gates remain incomplete.
- Themes that build on existing v0.2.0 modules score better than large platform expansions.
- AI Tutor remains important, but should be framed as preparation and risk reduction unless explicit scope is approved.
